Unfold Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2026

Unfold is a personal wellness companion. Your privacy matters. This policy explains what data the app handles.

Data we collect

None. Unfold does not collect, transmit, or store your personal data on any server.

Data on your device

Your affirmations, beliefs, rituals progress, and journal-style entries are saved only in your device's local storage (browser or app storage). This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export or share it yourself outside the app.

Third parties

Unfold does not use analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking SDKs.

Children

Unfold is not directed at children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy. Continued use of the app means you accept the updated policy.

Contact

Questions? Contact the app developer at the email listed on the Google Play store listing.
